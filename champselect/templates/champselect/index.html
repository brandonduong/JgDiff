Features

  • Calculate early matchups!

    Find out which champion should on average, be stronger in the early game
    (in 1v1s, 2v2s, etc).

  • Maximize your chance of victory!

    With this knowledge, you can maximize your chance of winning by adapting your early path and playstyle!

  • Test your knowledege!

    Try and predict the matchups before they are even calculated to challenge your overall game knowledge!

About

A big part of jungling is knowing both the jungle and lane matchups, of which can get difficult to remember, especially with League nearing ~150 champions! However, with JgDiff, you can quickly and accurately calculate yours and your team's points of power! Want to know which sides of the map you'll have lane priority to invade the enemy jungle? To secure that pesky scuttle crab? To bully their poor adc?

Well it's all just a couple clicks away!
