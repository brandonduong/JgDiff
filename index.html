Features

  • Calculate early matchups!

    Find out which champion should on average, be stronger in the early game
    (in 1v1s, 2v2s, etc).

  • Maximize your chance of victory!

  • Test your knowledege!

    Try and predict the matchups before they are even calculated to challenge your overall game knowledge!
